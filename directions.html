Directions to Make Empanadas

  • Heat a large skillet over medium heat. Add ground beef. Cook it completely, breaking it up as it cooks. Drain any excess grease after fully cooking it.

  • Add the parboiled diced potato, Goya sofrito, and ¼ cup water. Cook for 10 minutes, stirring often. After 10 minutes is up, stir in a can of sofrito, Goya Sazón con Azafran seasoning packet, and ¼ cup water. Stir and cook for 5 minutes.

  • Finally, add 20 chopped Spanish olives to the pan, and mix through.
