Empanadas



Growing up in NYC, I loved Puerto Rican Empanadas. Similar to turnovers, empanadas are filled with savory or sweet ingredients. This recipe is for the classic beef and potato. Some of my other favorite flavor varieties are: chicken, cheese and guava.

Empanadillas vs Pastelillos

Regionally on the island, Puerto Rican Empanadas are known as empanadillas or pastelillos. While this topic usually follows with an intense debate, for the purpose of this blog post, I'll say all names are right.
